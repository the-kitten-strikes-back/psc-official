Our Partnerships

The PSC is allied with multiple organizations, the most successful of all being JulianCO, the jewel trading card game.

Our Partner Organizations

1

JulianCO 💎

The PSC has collaborated with JulianCO as two emerging companies. As part of the sponsorship, we created a JulianCO Trading Cards game, which has been made into an online app as well. JulianCO is a fun, engaging game in which players are dealt packs of jewels. There are multiple rounds with...unexpected challenges and twists. So why wait? The Dealer's open. #PlayJulianCO

2

Balochistan Penil Mafia

The PSC has made many succesful trades with this enterprising organization that has risen up the ranks of indie pen collectors. We give it our full support and alliance, offering a generous amount of 20% of our pencil income as investment to this company.
Note: The region "Balochistan" was used purely for naming purposes and does not have any relation to the company's actual HQ location(s), interests, and views.
