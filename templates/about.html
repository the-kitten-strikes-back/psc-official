Who We Are

The It all started in 2025, when our founders had a dream. The class 8B was the birthplace of this idea, where creativity and innovation merged to form the foundation of what would become the Pen Stealing Company.

Our Story

Founded by a group of innovative students, PSC began as a simple idea: to create the ultimate writing experience. Through dedication, creativity, and a passion for excellence, we've grown from a small classroom initiative into a leading name in the stationary industry.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, strategic partnerships(like our latest one with the Balochistan Pencil Mafia), and an unwavering commitment. Today, PSC stands as a testament to what can be achieved through vision, hard work, and a love for the pen.

What We Do

At PSC, we specialize in:

  • Premium Pen Loaning - Providing high-quality pens for loan from our diverse collection
  • Classification and Categorization - Organizing and managing our product inventory
  • Systematic Collection of Pens - We have a vast collection of 200+ pens in our (highly secure :D) storage facilities.
