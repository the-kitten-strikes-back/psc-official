Our Mission

To revolutionize the stationary industry through innovation, creativity, and a commitment to excellence in pen technology and distribution.

Who We Are

It all started in 2025, when our founders had a dream. The class 8B was the birthplace of this idea, where creativity and innovation merged to form the foundation of what would become the Pen Stealing Company.

Our Story

Founded by a group of innovative students, PSC began as a simple idea: to create the ultimate writing experience.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, strategic partnerships (like our latest one with the Balochistan Pencil Mafia), and an unwavering commitment.

What We Do

At PSC, we specialize in:

  • Premium Pen Loaning — Providing high-quality pens for loan.
  • Classification and Categorization — Organizing and managing our inventory.
  • Systematic Collection of Pens — Over 200+ pens in secure storage.

Our Values

Resilience: Constant improvement.

Quality: Never compromise.
