Why Not Loanable?
Class S pens exist solely for observation and historical preservation. Allowing them into active rotation would be akin to lending the Gutenberg Bible. The ink has crystallized into museum-grade preservation fluid. The mechanisms are sealed in hermetic chambers. To loan one would be to risk shattering a thousand-year dream, pen by precious pen.
